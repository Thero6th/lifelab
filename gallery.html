TOP ROCK

Any movement that is performed on your feet. Usually the introduction to a dancers set

DOWN ROCK

Any movement that is performed on your feet as well as your hands. Your hands and your feet must be placed onto the floor. This movement is usually placed into a set for power and freeze transitions.

FREEZE

Any movement that allows the dancer to place themselves into a plank. The only move that possesses the least amount of motion.

POWER

Any movement that is performed with massive amount of balance and/or power. The only move that possesses the most amount of movement.

AERIAL

Any movement that is performed in mid air.

TRANSITIONS

Any movement that is performed with the intention of connecting other moves. This is the space in breaking in which new moves and sets are created.
